Space has a smell

Astronauts say that after going into outer space, their spacesuits smell something like metal, burnt steak, or hot metal. This is due to ozone and ions that are formed in the vacuum.

Stars don't twinkle in space

They twinkle only for us on Earth because the light is distorted by the atmosphere. In space, stars shine steadily.

The Moon is moving away

Every year, the Moon moves away from us by about 3.8 cm. It used to be much closer and looked gigantic.
